Making Dashing Magazine

While choosing the contents in this magazine, I made sure that there were no stories or whatsoever that were age-inappropriate and violated Pisay's Code of Conduct. I've never particularly been a fan of magazines myself since I don't browse that much on Google nor have I ever collected any magazines, but I will say that the process of making this online magazine was fun despite the stress while making it.
